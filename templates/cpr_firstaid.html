How to Perform CPR

Step-by-step — adult, child, and infant

📞
1. Call 911
Call immediately or have someone else call
🤲
2. Chest Compressions
100–120 per minute, 2 inches deep
💨
3. Rescue Breaths
2 breaths after every 30 compressions
⚡
4. Use AED
If available — follow the prompts
